# WedPlan Mobile App User Manual

**Version 1.0.0**

This guide explains how to use WedPlan Mobile App as a couple, vendor, or guest.

## 1. Starting the App

When you open WedPlan, you will first see the splash screen. The app checks whether you already have a saved session.

- If you are already signed in, the app opens your dashboard.
- If you are not signed in, the app shows the welcome screen.

## 2. Welcome Screen

The welcome screen is the starting point for new users.

You can choose one of these actions:

- Couple Registration: create a couple account
- Vendor Registration: create a vendor account
- Login: sign in to an existing account
- Guest Invitation: open the guest invitation flow

The welcome screen also highlights the main planning experience so users can understand the app before registering.

## 3. Couple User Guide

Use the couple flow if you are planning a wedding and want to manage all event details in one place.

### 3.1 Couple Dashboard

The dashboard gives a quick overview of wedding planning activity. From here you can jump into the main planning areas.

### 3.2 Budget

Use the Budget tab to:

- Review your overall wedding budget
- Track category allocations
- Monitor spending progress
- Check whether a category is over or under plan

### 3.3 Vendors

Use the Vendors tab to:

- Browse vendor information
- Review vendor-related planning details
- Move into booking-related views when needed

### 3.4 Guest List

Use the Guest tab to:

- Manage invited guests
- Review invitation and RSVP progress
- Check guest status where available

### 3.5 Tasks

Use the task screen to:

- Add planning tasks
- Review the task list
- Mark tasks as completed
- Keep your wedding checklist organized

### 3.6 Notifications

Use the notifications button to review updates and important planning alerts.

### 3.7 Me / Profile

Use the Me tab to:

- Review account details
- Update profile-related settings
- Manage couple-specific information

## 4. Vendor User Guide

Use the vendor flow if you provide wedding-related services.

### 4.1 Vendor Dashboard

The vendor dashboard shows business activity and quick access to key work areas.

### 4.2 Service

Use the Service tab to:

- Manage service offerings
- Review service data
- Update business-related items tied to your profile

### 4.3 Booking

Use the Booking tab to:

- Review upcoming bookings
- Track client appointments
- Monitor booking activity and status

### 4.4 Notifications

Use the notifications icon to stay up to date with vendor alerts and booking-related updates.

### 4.5 Me / Profile

Use the Me tab to:

- Review account details
- Update business profile information
- Manage your vendor profile photo or related settings if supported by the backend

## 5. Guest User Guide

Use the guest flow if you have received a wedding invitation.

### 5.1 Invitation Lookup

Open the guest invitation screen and enter the invitation code or QR-related value provided by the couple.

### 5.2 RSVP / Check-In

After the invitation is found, you can view the invitation details and complete the available RSVP or check-in action.

## 6. Common Tasks

### Register as a Couple

1. Open the welcome screen.
2. Tap Couple Registration.
3. Fill in the registration form.
4. Submit the form to create your account.

### Register as a Vendor

1. Open the welcome screen.
2. Tap Vendor Registration.
3. Enter your business details.
4. Submit the form to create your vendor account.

### Log In

1. Open the welcome screen.
2. Tap Login.
3. Enter your account credentials.
4. Submit the form to open your dashboard.

### Open a Guest Invitation

1. Open the welcome screen.
2. Tap Guest Invitation.
3. Enter or scan the invitation reference.
4. Review the invitation details and continue.

## 7. Tips for Best Results

- Use the latest version of Flutter-supported mobile browsers or devices for the smoothest experience.
- Make sure your device has network access so the app can load live planning data.
- Allow notification permissions if you want to receive timely updates.
- Keep your session signed in on a trusted device for faster access.

## 8. Troubleshooting

### The app stays on the splash screen

- Check your internet connection.
- Close and reopen the app.
- Sign in again if your saved session has expired.

### Login does not work

- Verify your email and password.
- Make sure the backend service is available.
- Try again after checking your connection.

### Invitations do not load

- Confirm the invitation code or QR value is correct.
- Make sure the invitation has not been removed or expired.

### Notifications are missing

- Allow notification permissions on your device.
- Check that push notifications are enabled for the app.
